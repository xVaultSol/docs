The first xVault release is built around a small set of curated, governance-defined baskets rather than user-generated vaults.

## Planned SKUs

| Vault | Ticker | Composition | Weighting | Rebalance trigger |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Mag 7 | `vMAG7` | AAPLx, MSFTx, NVDAx, GOOGLx, AMZNx, METAx, TSLAx | Equal-weight | 3% drift or 7 days |
| Semis | `vCHIP` | NVDAx, AMDx, TSMx, AVGOx, INTCx | Market-cap | 3% drift or 7 days |
| Dividend | `vDIV` | KOx, JNJx, PGx, XOMx | Equal-weight | 5% drift or 30 days |
| SPY-Lite | `vSPY` | SPYx plus USDC buffer | Fixed 95/5 | 2% drift |

## Why curated vaults

- Weight definitions stay auditable.
- Corporate-action response stays operationally manageable.
- Keeper policies can be tailored per vault basket.
- Governance retains control over index quality and replacement logic.

## Pause conditions

Certain corporate actions require vault intervention rather than automatic continuation. Spin-offs, merger variants, redemption events, worthless removals, and rights distributions should pause affected vaults until multisig review is complete.
