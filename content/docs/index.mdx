title: Welcome to xVault
description: Onchain index vaults of tokenized equities on Solana, with $VLT rewards paid in real xStock kind.
---

xVault is a Solana protocol that issues **auto-rebalancing index vaults** of tokenized equities (xStocks) and distributes protocol revenue to **$VLT stakers** in real equity kind rather than inflationary emissions.

Deposit USDC into a curated basket — Mag 7, Semiconductors, Dividend, SPY-Lite — and receive a single Token-2022 share that tracks the basket's NAV. A keeper rebalances to target weights through Backed's xChange during market hours and Jupiter off-hours, passing corporate actions through as rewards.

                  ┌──────────────────────────────┐
  User (USDC) ───▶│   Next.js dapp + SDK         │
                  └───────────────┬──────────────┘
                                  │
                                  ▼
        ┌──────────────────────────────────────────────┐
        │    Solana programs — vault · oracle · rewards│
        └───────────────┬──────────────────────────────┘
                        │
         ┌──────────────┼────────────────┐
         ▼              ▼                ▼
   xStocks API     Jupiter          Pyth / oracle
